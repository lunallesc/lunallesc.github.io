Final Project

Info

Our program is a game called Number Treasure Hunt. The goal of the game is to collect as many points as possible
and end up with the higher score than your opponent. Younger players who play this game can also learn how to add
both positive and negative numbers.

Instructions

The goal of the game is simple, try to dig up more valuable treasure
than your opponent. Player 1 gets first move off the middle tile,
either up or down! The second player then gets to move side to side
within the row of your oponents last move after no more legal moves can be made
it's a free for all. The game ends when no more moves can be made.
Good Luck!
